History

We want to provide a non-intrusive resource that people who feel isolated will want to use. Designed to be a safe, secure place to express yourself, the Glass Bottle allows users to write about their feelings anonymously and then automatically deletes any text typed into its field. This is perfect for those struggling with depression who may not feel confident enough to confide in a real person. Our website then helps these individuals to gain confidence and recuperate by offering increasing levels of sharing: users can first vent without saving, then they can post anonymously to support others in their positions, and lastly, can join anonymous chats where they discuss their issues with real people who have struggled with the same issues.

How it Works

How does the Glass Bottle work? The text you type isn’t even sent to our page. It exists on the surface of your computer and disappears as soon as you press “send”. Doesn’t even cross the internet or leave your home, unless you choose so: if you do feel like sharing your writing with people in similar situations, you can log in and press “post” to share your anonymous thoughts to positively impact others.
